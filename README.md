# Sales Performance & Analytics Dashboard

##  Project Overview
An interactive, end-to-end 5-Page Power BI Dashboard designed to analyze retail transaction data, track sales performance, understand customer demographics, and evaluate payment behaviors. The project transforms raw transaction logs into actionable business intelligence.

##  Tech Stack Used
* **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing:** Microsoft Excel 
* **Data Modeling & Visualization:** Power BI Desktop

## Key Insights & Features

### 1. Executive Sales Overview
* **Revenue Dominance:** Electronics leads the store's sales contribution at 55.06%, followed by Furniture at 38.96%.
* **Core Metrics:** Dynamically tracks Average Revenue (7.33K), Total Quantities (990), and Order counts (360).
* **Trend Analysis:** Area chart tracks continuous daily sales flow and performance peaks.

### 2. Product & Category Performance
* **Top Driver:** Laptops emerge as the single highest revenue-generating individual product.
* **Volume Leader:** Office Supplies accounts for the maximum transaction volume (pieces sold).

### 3. Geographic Hubs & Customers
* **Top Region:** Mumbai stands out as the highest revenue-generating tier-1 city, followed by Pune and Jaipur.
* **Granular Tracking:** Fully equipped with a customer-to-city ledger tracking buyer specifics.

### 4. Payment Analytics
* **Digital Preference:** UPI holds the maximum visual share in sales distribution, indicating high adoption of digital modes for high-ticket orders.
* **Cash Behavior:** Cash transactions are frequent but mostly limited to smaller ticket items.

### 5. Grand Data Audit Table
* Includes a 100% full-width tabular ledger for end-users and managers to audit line-item transactions row-by-row.
